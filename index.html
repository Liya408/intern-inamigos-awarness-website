Our Story

Get to Know Us Better

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government of India. Based in Chhattisgarh, we work tirelessly across the nation to uplift communities through collective compassion and action.

We hold prestigious certifications including 80G & 12A, CSR-1 registration, NITI Aayog registration, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 — ensuring the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and impact delivery.

🎯

Empower underprivileged communities through education, welfare, and sustainable development across India.
